## Summary  
- **Objective**: Introduce Test-time Recursive Thinking (TRT) as a novel framework for multi-round reasoning in competitive programming and mathematical problem-solving, aiming to improve solution quality through iterative refinement and knowledge accumulation.  
- **Methodologies**: TRT employs three core components—Solver Agent (iterative problem-solving with self-correction), Knowledge Manager (compressed insight extraction from past attempts), and Test Generator (targeted test-case creation to expose bugs). It compares against Markovian Thinking, which relies on appending previous answers to context.  
- **Results**: TRT achieves consistent, monotonic improvements across three independent runs on AIME-25, outperforming baselines like Majority@64. For example, gpt-oss-120b and Qwen3-235B achieve 100% accuracy, while Markovian methods plateau at ~93%. TRT’s compressed knowledge extraction (1.5% of context) enables stable, context-agnostic performance.  
- **Key Contributions**:  
  1. Structured, iterative refinement framework (Solver, Knowledge Manager, Test Generator).  
  2. Compressed knowledge extraction to retain critical insights without context dilution.  
  3. Empirical validation of TRT’s superiority over Markovian Thinking in multi-round reasoning.  
- **Conclusions**: TRT provides a robust, reproducible method for enhancing problem-solving accuracy through recursive knowledge refinement, demonstrating strong generalization and stability across problem domains.

## 摘要  
- **目标**：介绍Test-time Recursive Thinking（TRT）作为一项针对竞赛编程和数学问题求解的多轮推理新框架，旨在通过迭代优化和知识积累提升解决方案的质量。  
- **方法**：TRT采用三个核心组件——求解代理（通过自我修正进行迭代问题求解）、知识管理器（从过往尝试中提取压缩洞察）和测试生成器（创建针对性测试用例以暴露错误）。它与依赖上下文追加历史答案的马尔可夫推理方法进行对比。  
- **结果**：TRT在AIME-25的三组独立运行中实现了一致且单调的性能提升，优于基线方法如Majority@64。例如，gpt-oss-120b和Qwen3-235B达到100%准确率，而马尔可夫方法则稳定在约93%。TRT的压缩知识提取（仅占上下文的1.5%）实现了稳定、与上下文无关的性能。  
- **关键贡献**：  
  1. 结构化、迭代式优化框架（求解代理、知识管理器、测试生成器）。  
  2. 压缩知识提取以保留关键洞察，避免上下文稀释。  
  3. 通过多轮推理验证TRT相较于马尔可夫推理的优越性。  
- **结论**：TRT提供了一种稳健、可复现的方法，通过递归知识优化提升问题求解准确率，在不同问题领域中展现出强大的泛化能力和稳定性。
