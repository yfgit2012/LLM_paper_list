## Summary
- **Objective**: To develop a structured, iterative framework (Test-time Recursive Thinking, TRT) for solving complex problems like competitive programming and mathematical reasoning tasks (e.g., AIME) by integrating critical analysis, knowledge accumulation, and systematic refinement.  
- **Methodologies**: TRT employs four core components: (1) a **Solver Agent** to generate and verify code solutions, (2) a **Knowledge Manager** to curate actionable insights from past attempts, (3) a **Test Generator** to create edge-case test scenarios, and (4) **System Prompts** to enforce structured output formats. The framework iteratively refines solutions through problem-solving, knowledge accumulation, and test validation, contrasting with Markovian Thinking by avoiding context dilution.  
- **Results**: TRT achieves reproducible, efficient, and robust performance (e.g., 100% accuracy in AIME-25 tasks), with compressed knowledge reducing context overhead while maintaining accuracy. It outperforms Markovian approaches by enabling stable improvements beyond large context limits.  
- **Key Contributions**:  
  1. Introduces TRT as a novel framework for iterative problem-solving in complex domains.  
  2. Proposes structured knowledge accumulation to avoid redundant mistakes and guide future attempts.  
  3. Demonstrates the superiority of TRT over Markovian Thinking in handling large contexts and edge cases.  
- **Conclusions**: TRT provides a scalable, systematic approach to solving complex problems by combining iterative refinement, structured knowledge management, and targeted testing, making it ideal for competitive programming and mathematical reasoning tasks.

## 摘要
- **目标**：开发一个结构化、迭代化的框架（测试时递归思维，TRT），通过整合批判性分析、知识积累和系统化优化，解决复杂问题（如编程竞赛和数学推理任务，例如AIME）。  
- **方法**：TRT包含四个核心组件：(1) **求解代理** 用于生成和验证代码解决方案，(2) **知识管理器** 用于整理过往尝试中的可操作见解，(3) **测试生成器** 用于创建边界测试场景，(4) **系统提示** 用于强制结构化输出格式。该框架通过问题解决、知识积累和测试验证迭代优化解决方案，与马尔可夫思维不同，避免了上下文稀释问题。  
- **结果**：TRT实现了可复现、高效且稳健的性能（例如AIME-25任务100%准确率），通过压缩知识减少上下文开销同时保持准确性。其性能优于马尔可夫方法，能够突破大上下文限制实现稳定提升。  
- **关键贡献**：  
  1. 引入TRT作为复杂领域迭代问题解决的新框架。  
  2. 提出结构化知识积累方法，避免重复错误并指导未来尝试。  
  3. 证明TRT在处理大上下文和边界情况时优于马尔可夫思维。  
- **结论**：TRT通过结合迭代优化、结构化知识管理及针对性测试，提供了一种可扩展、系统化的复杂问题解决方法，适用于编程竞赛和数学推理任务。
