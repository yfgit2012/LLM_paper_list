## Summary  
- **Objective**: The paper aims to develop a structured framework for generating, refining, and evaluating rubrics to ensure precision, objectivity, and actionable criteria for assessing responses to prompts.  
- **Methodologies**: The approach involves three stages: (1) **Initial Rubric Generation** (creating baseline rubrics from prompts and example responses), (2) **Rubric Decomposition** (refining broad criteria into granular, non-overlapping sub-criteria), and (3) **Rubric Validation** (checking for conflicts or overlaps). Additionally, **Response Evaluation** is used to assess whether a response satisfies a rubric. All stages emphasize strict formatting and binary, specific criteria.  
- **Results**: The framework enables the creation of precise, actionable rubrics (e.g., "Correctly identifies the chemical equation of photosynthesis") and avoids ambiguity through overlap/conflict checks. Examples demonstrate how broad criteria are decomposed into specific, verifiable checks, enhancing coverage and granularity.  
- **Key Contributions**:  
  1. A systematic approach to rubric generation and refinement.  
  2. Recursive decomposition of broad criteria into sub-dimensions (e.g., "Correctness of equations" vs. "Mentions all inputs").  
  3. Validation mechanisms to prevent overlapping or conflicting criteria.  
  4. Strict formatting guidelines for `<RUBRIC>` and `<EVALUATION>` tags to ensure consistency.  
- **Conclusions**: The framework ensures rubrics are precise, free from ambiguity, and aligned with specific, verifiable criteria, enabling effective and objective evaluation of responses.

## 摘要  
- **目标**：本文旨在构建一个结构化框架，用于生成、优化和评估评分标准，以确保对提示响应的评估具备精确性、客观性和可操作性标准。  
- **方法**：该方法包含三个阶段：(1) **初始评分标准生成**（从提示和示例响应中创建基准评分标准），(2) **评分标准分解**（将宽泛标准细化为非重叠的子标准），(3) **评分标准验证**（检查是否存在冲突或重叠）。此外，**响应评估**用于判断响应是否符合评分标准。所有阶段均强调严格格式化和二元、具体的评估标准。  
- **结果**：该框架能够生成精确且可操作的评分标准（例如：“正确识别光合作用的化学方程式”），并通过重叠/冲突检查避免歧义。示例展示了宽泛标准如何被细化为具体、可验证的检查项，从而提升覆盖范围和细致程度。  
- **关键贡献**：  
  1. 评分标准生成与优化的系统化方法。  
  2. 将宽泛标准递归分解为子维度（例如：“方程的正确性” vs. “提及所有输入”）。  
  3. 防止标准重叠或冲突的验证机制。  
  4. 对 `<RUBRIC>` 和 `<EVALUATION>` 标签的严格格式化指南，以确保一致性。  
- **结论**：该框架确保评分标准具备精确性、无歧义性，并与具体、可验证的标准对齐，从而实现响应评估的有效性和客观性。
