## Summary  
- **Objective**: To develop an efficient, scalable method for editing pre-trained language models (model editing) that outperforms existing approaches like MEMIT in accuracy and runtime efficiency.  
- **Methodologies**:  
  - Utilizes **null-space projection** to modify the hidden dimensions of pre-trained models ($ d_0 $) without dependency on model size, layers, or knowledge base scale.  
  - Employs **runtime optimization** to minimize computational overhead, ensuring scalability for large models.  
  - Evaluates performance on factual correction (Counterfact) and relational/editing tasks (ZsRE) datasets.  
- **Results**:  
  - Achieves **higher editing accuracy** than MEMIT on Counterfact (e.g., 98.94% on ZsRE for GPT-J) and ZsRE datasets.  
  - Maintains **competitive runtime** (334–488 seconds for 100 edits) compared to MEMIT (222–476 seconds), with scalability emphasized as a key advantage.  
  - Demonstrates **robustness** and practicality for large-scale model editing.  
- **Key Contributions**:  
  1. Introduces **null-space projection** for efficient, model-agnostic editing.  
  2. Achieves **runtime efficiency** and **scalability** without sacrificing accuracy.  
  3. Outperforms MEMIT in **editing accuracy** and **task-specific performance** (e.g., factual updates).  
- **Conclusions**: AlphaEdit provides a practical, efficient solution for model editing, balancing computational cost and performance. It is well-suited for real-world applications requiring frequent model updates, such as bias correction or knowledge refinement.

## 摘要  
- **目标**：开发一种高效、可扩展的预训练语言模型编辑方法（模型编辑），在准确率和运行时效率方面超越现有方法如MEMIT。  
- **方法**：  
  - 采用**空空间投影**技术，无需依赖模型规模、层数或知识库规模，即可修改预训练模型的隐藏维度（$ d_0 $）。  
  - 通过**运行时优化**减少计算开销，确保大模型的可扩展性。  
  - 在事实修正（Counterfact）和关系/编辑任务（ZsRE）数据集上评估性能。  
- **结果**：  
  - 在Counterfact数据集（如ZsRE上GPT-J的98.94%）和ZsRE数据集上，编辑准确率高于MEMIT。  
  - 与MEMIT（222–476秒）相比，**运行时间具有竞争力**（100次编辑需334–488秒），可扩展性是其关键优势。  
  - 展示了**鲁棒性**和大规模模型编辑的实用性。  
- **关键贡献**：  
  1. 引入**空空间投影**，实现高效、与模型无关的编辑。  
  2. 在不牺牲准确率的前提下，实现**运行时效率**和**可扩展性**。  
  3. 在**编辑准确率**和**任务特定性能**（如事实更新）方面优于MEMIT。  
- **结论**：AlphaEdit为模型编辑提供了一种实用且高效的解决方案，平衡了计算成本与性能。其适用于需要频繁更新模型的实际应用，如偏差修正或知识优化。
