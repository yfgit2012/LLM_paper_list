## Summary  
- **Objective**: To compare the performance of AI models (GPT-5.2, GEMINI-3 PRO, CLAUDE-4.5 SONNET) in text-based and vision-based environments, focusing on exploration strategies, efficiency, and task-specific capabilities.  
- **Methodologies**: Structured testing in two-room and four-room layouts with passive vs. active exploration scenarios. Tasks included spatial reasoning (e.g., perspective taking, mental rotation) and strict answer format requirements (e.g., coordinate pairs, action sequences). Visualization tools like cognitive maps and trajectory analysis were used to evaluate exploration patterns.  
- **Results**:  
  - GPT-5.2 excelled in text-based environments but struggled with vision-based tasks.  
  - GEMINI-3 PRO demonstrated systematic exploration strategies (e.g., door-finding, sweeping patterns) across both modalities.  
  - CLAUDE-4.5 SONNET lacked clear exploration patterns, leading to inefficient navigation.  
  - Active exploration was critical in four-room settings, where efficiency directly impacted performance.  
- **Key Contributions**:  
  - Comparative analysis of modality-specific strengths and weaknesses of AI models.  
  - Identification of exploration strategies (e.g., strategic rotation, pivoting) as critical for complex environments.  
  - Development of visualization tools (cognitive maps, trajectory analysis) to assess model behavior.  
- **Conclusions**: Context-aware exploration and modality-specific strategies are essential for AI systems in dynamic environments. Active exploration outperforms passive observation in larger spaces, and improving systematic navigation (e.g., for CLAUDE-4.5 SONNET) is a key area for future research.

## 摘要  
- **目标**：比较AI模型（GPT-5.2、GEMINI-3 PRO、CLAUDE-4.5 SONNET）在基于文本和基于视觉环境中的表现，重点关注探索策略、效率及任务特定能力。  
- **方法**：在双房间和四房间布局中进行结构化测试，包含被动与主动探索场景。任务包括空间推理（如视角转换、心理旋转）及严格答案格式要求（如坐标对、动作序列）。使用认知地图和轨迹分析等可视化工具评估探索模式。  
- **结果**：  
  - GPT-5.2在基于文本环境中表现优异，但在基于视觉任务中表现不佳。  
  - GEMINI-3 PRO在两种模式中均展现出系统化的探索策略（如门寻找、扫视模式）。  
  - CLAUDE-4.5 SONNET缺乏明确的探索模式，导致导航效率低下。  
  - 在四房间场景中，主动探索至关重要，效率直接影响性能。  
- **关键贡献**：  
  - 分析AI模型在不同模态中的特定优势与不足。  
  - 识别探索策略（如战略旋转、转向）对复杂环境的重要性。  
  - 开发可视化工具（认知地图、轨迹分析）以评估模型行为。  
- **结论**：在动态环境中，AI系统需具备情境感知的探索能力及模态特定策略。主动探索在更大空间中优于被动观察，提升系统化导航（如CLAUDE-4.5 SONNET）是未来研究的关键方向。
