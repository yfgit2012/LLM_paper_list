### **Information Augmentation Details**  
This section outlines the implementation of state augmentation for **ALFWorld**, **Webshop**, and **Sokoban**, including synthetic distractors, templates, and parameter controls.  

---

### **C.1 ALFWorld**  
**Goal**: Enhance state richness with synthetic distractors that do not interfere with task completion.  
- **Distractor Objects**:  
  - **Types**: Sampled from `{bowl, cup, pan, ...}` (predefined list).  
  - **Descriptors**: Randomly selected from `{cracked, dirty, slightly burnt, ...}`.  
  - **Templates**: Combined using phrases like *"You notice a [object] that looks [descriptor]"* or *"Near the sink, there is a [object] that is [descriptor]"*.  
- **Augmentation Volume**:  
  - Controlled by parameter **_ϵ_** (e.g., _ϵ_ = 120 adds ~10 sentences, _ϵ_ = 360 adds ~30).  
  - Each sentence is ~12 tokens long, so **_n_ distractive = ⌊_ϵ_/12⌋** sentences are injected per state.  

---

### **C.2 Webshop**  
**Goal**: Inject irrelevant advertising or trivial product info into product-detail and search-result pages.  
- **Product-Details Pages**:  
  - **Trivial Features**: Sampled from `{fabric: machine wash cold, shipping note: ships within 5-7 days, ...}`.  
  - **Templates**: Added as standalone sentences (e.g., *"Model is 5’8” wearing size medium"*).  
- **Search-Result Pages**:  
  - **Trivial Products**: Non-targeted product types paired with features like `{sleeveless, out of stock, missing product images, ...}`.  
  - **Templates**: Presented as warnings or ads (e.g., *"Warning: [desc] [obj] (cannot be selected)"*).  
- **Augmentation Volume**:  
  - **_ϵ_** controls the number of inserted items/sentences:  
    - **Search pages**: Up to 10 additional slots filled with **_k_ = min(⌊_ϵ_/100 * α · 10⌋, 10)** entries.  
    - **Detail pages**: **_k_ = max(1, ⌊_ϵ_/25⌋)** feature sentences and **_k_ = max(1, ⌊_ϵ_/30⌋)** ad sentences.  

---

### **C.3 Sokoban**  
**Goal**: Add unreachable locations with synthetic obstacles to the grid description.  
- **Distractor Elements**:  
  - **Objects**: Sampled from `{Broken crate, Sealed door, Pile of rubble, ...}`.  
  - **Descriptions**: Phrases like *"outside the room"*, *"beyond the wall"*, or *"on a blocked corridor"*.  
- **Templates**: Combined as *(r, c) shows a [obj] ([desc]; unreachable)*.  
- **Augmentation Volume**:  
  - Controlled by **_ϵ_** (e.g., _ϵ_ = 50 adds ~5 lines, _ϵ_ = 150 adds ~15).  
  - Each sentence is ~10 tokens long, so **_k_ = max(1, ⌊_ϵ_/10⌋)** lines are appended.  
  - All distractors are explicitly placed **outside the grid**.  

---

### **C.4 Information Augmentation Volume Selection**  
The following table summarizes the **_ϵ_** values and augmentation probabilities used in experiments:  

| **Initial Policy** | **Training Domain** | **Information Augmentation Volume** (_ϵ_) | **Probability of Applying Augmentation** |  
|--------------------|---------------------|-------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------|  
| Ckpt V1           | ALFWorld            | 300                                       | 100%                                     |  
| Ckpt V1           | Webshop             | 30                                        | 100%                                     |  
| Ckpt V1           | Sokoban             | 80                                        | 100%                                     |  
| Ckpt V2           | ALFWorld            | 200                                       | 50%                                      |  
| Ckpt V2           | Webshop             | 40                                        | 50%                                      |  
| Ckpt V2           | Sokoban             | 80                                        | 50%                                      |  

--- 

This approach ensures diverse and controlled augmentation, balancing task fidelity with complexity.
