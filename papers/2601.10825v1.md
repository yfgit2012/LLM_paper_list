## Summary  
- **Objective**: To analyze the relationships between DeepSeek-R1, conversational behaviors, socio-emotional roles, cognitive reasoning strategies, and task accuracy using structural equation modeling (SEM) to identify key predictors of performance in task-based evaluations.  
- **Methodologies**: The study employs SEM to quantify direct and indirect effects of variables (e.g., cognitive strategies, conversational behaviors) on accuracy. Task fixed effects are controlled for, and IFEval tasks are excluded due to evaluation challenges.  
- **Results**:  
  - DeepSeek-R1 shows strong positive effects on multiple outcomes (z-scores > 60, p < 0.001) and directly impacts accuracy (β = 0.253, p < 0.001).  
  - Cognitive strategies like backward chaining (β = 0.005, p < 0.001) and subgoal setting (β = 0.009, p = 0.009) enhance accuracy, while backtracking (β = -0.003, p = 0.005) and conflict of perspectives (β = -0.006, p = 0.001) reduce it.  
  - Conversational behaviors such as question-and-answering (β = 0.009, p < 0.001) positively predict accuracy, whereas perspective shift (β = -0.006, p < 0.001) and conflict of perspectives negatively affect it.  
  - Socio-emotional roles like "Give" and "Ask" show weak or non-significant associations with accuracy.  
- **Key Contributions**:  
  - Development of an SEM framework to integrate cognitive, conversational, and socio-emotional factors in task performance analysis.  
  - Identification of specific cognitive strategies and conversational behaviors that significantly influence accuracy, offering actionable insights for AI/ML system design.  
- **Conclusions**: DeepSeek-R1 is a critical determinant of accuracy, but task performance is also shaped by balancing cognitive strategies (e.g., backward chaining) and conversational dynamics. These findings highlight the need for systems that harmonize reasoning processes with social interaction patterns to optimize task outcomes.

## 摘要  
- **目标**：利用结构方程模型（SEM）分析DeepSeek-R1、对话行为、情感社会角色、认知推理策略与任务准确率之间的关系，以识别任务评估中表现的关键预测因素。  
- **方法**：研究采用SEM量化变量（如认知策略、对话行为）对准确率的直接和间接效应。控制任务固定效应，并因评估挑战排除IFEval任务。  
- **结果**：  
  - DeepSeek-R1对多个结果表现出显著正效应（z值>60，p<0.001），并直接提升准确率（β=0.253，p<0.001）。  
  - 认知策略如逆向链式推理（β=0.005，p<0.001）和子目标设定（β=0.009，p=0.009）提高准确率，而回溯（β=-0.003，p=0.005）和观点冲突（β=-0.006，p=0.001）则降低准确率。  
  - 对话行为如问答（β=0.009，p<0.001）正向预测准确率，而观点转换（β=-0.006，p<0.001）和观点冲突则负向影响准确率。  
  - 情感社会角色如“给予”和“询问”与准确率的关联较弱或不显著。  
- **关键贡献**：  
  - 构建了一个整合认知、对话和情感社会因素的SEM框架，用于任务表现分析。  
  - 识别了显著影响准确率的具体认知策略和对话行为，为AI/ML系统设计提供可操作的见解。  
- **结论**：DeepSeek-R1是准确率的关键决定因素，但任务表现还受到平衡认知策略（如逆向链式推理）和对话动态的影响。这些发现强调了需要协调推理过程与社交互动模式的系统，以优化任务结果。
