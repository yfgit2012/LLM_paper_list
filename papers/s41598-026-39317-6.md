## Summary  
- **Objective**: To investigate the impact of AI-based educational tools, particularly large language model (LLM)-driven systems, on student engagement, empathy, and nature-relatedness, while addressing ethical and methodological considerations in AI-driven education.  
- **Methodologies**: The study employed LLM-based AI systems, immersive VR environments (e.g., simulating nature experiences like "embodying a tree"), and reflective writing exercises. Statistical analysis was used to evaluate correlations between AI interactions, empathy, and nature-relatedness. Ethical compliance was ensured through informed consent, data anonymization, and adherence to guidelines from the Helsinki Declaration and German Data Forum.  
- **Results**: Immersive VR significantly enhanced participants’ nature-relatedness, as demonstrated in prior work. LLMs showed potential to foster empathy through human-like interactions, though design specifics influenced outcomes. Ethical frameworks and transparency were identified as critical for responsible AI integration in education.  
- **Key Contributions**: The paper links AI tools to both empathy and environmental awareness, offering insights into immersive technologies’ role in education. It also emphasizes ethical standards and methodological rigor in AI research, providing a framework for future studies.  
- **Conclusions**: AI-based systems can effectively enhance empathy and nature-relatedness in educational settings, but their design must prioritize transparency, informed consent, and human oversight to mitigate ethical risks.

## 摘要  
- **目标**：探讨基于人工智能的教育工具，特别是由大型语言模型（LLM）驱动的系统，对学生参与度、同理心和自然相关性的影响，同时解决人工智能驱动教育中的伦理和方法论问题。  
- **方法**：研究采用基于LLM的人工智能系统、沉浸式虚拟现实环境（如模拟自然体验的“化身树木”），以及反思性写作练习。通过统计分析评估人工智能互动、同理心和自然相关性之间的相关性。伦理合规性通过知情同意、数据匿名化以及遵循赫尔辛基宣言和德国数据论坛指南得以保障。  
- **结果**：沉浸式虚拟现实显著增强了参与者对自然的联系，如先前研究所示。LLM通过类人交互展现出培养同理心的潜力，但设计细节影响了结果。伦理框架和透明度被认定为负责任地将人工智能整合到教育中的关键因素。  
- **主要贡献**：本文将人工智能工具与同理心和环境意识联系起来，提供了关于沉浸式技术在教育中作用的见解。同时强调人工智能研究中的伦理标准和方法论严谨性，为未来研究提供了框架。  
- **结论**：基于人工智能的系统可以在教育环境中有效增强同理心和自然相关性，但其设计必须优先考虑透明度、知情同意和人类监督，以减轻伦理风险。
