## Summary
- **Objective**: To develop a model (COGROUTER) that dynamically adjusts cognitive depth (reasoning levels) to balance accuracy and efficiency in task completion, addressing the limitations of fixed-depth or unstructured reasoning approaches.  
- **Methodologies**: The study compares COGROUTER with baselines (fixed cognitive levels and FreeForm) using reinforcement learning (GRPO/GiGPO) and adaptive thinking. It employs hyperparameter tuning and evaluates performance on benchmarks like ALFWorld and ScienceWorld.  
- **Results**: COGROUTER achieves 92.5% success rate with 1,739.42 tokens, outperforming fixed-level models (*L1*: 76.5% SR at 357.04 tokens; *L4*: 86.5% SR at 4,640.98 tokens) and FreeForm (81.5% SR at 4,068.88 tokens). Adaptive reasoning balances shallow and deep steps, optimizing efficiency and accuracy.  
- **Key Contributions**: Introduces adaptive cognitive depth adjustment, demonstrates its superiority in efficiency-accuracy trade-offs, and provides hyperparameter consistency for fair model comparisons. Highlights scalability to complex environments.  
- **Conclusions**: Adaptive thinking (COGROUTER) significantly improves task completion efficiency and accuracy compared to fixed or unstructured methods, emphasizing the value of dynamic reasoning in resource-sensitive tasks.

## 摘要
- **目标**：开发一种模型（COGROUTER），通过动态调整认知深度（推理层级）在任务完成中平衡准确性和效率，解决固定深度或非结构化推理方法的局限性。  
- **方法**：研究使用强化学习（GRPO/GiGPO）和自适应思维，将COGROUTER与基线模型（固定认知层级和FreeForm）进行对比。通过超参数调优，并在ALFWorld和ScienceWorld等基准测试中评估性能。  
- **结果**：COGROUTER以1,739.42个token实现92.5%的成功率，优于固定层级模型（*L1*：357.04个token时76.5%的成功率；*L4*：4,640.98个token时86.5%的成功率）和FreeForm（4,068.88个token时81.5%的成功率）。自适应推理在浅层和深层步骤之间取得平衡，优化效率与准确性。  
- **关键贡献**：引入自适应认知深度调整，证明其在效率-准确性权衡中的优越性，并提供超参数一致性以实现公平的模型对比。强调其在复杂环境中的可扩展性。  
- **结论**：相比固定或非结构化方法，自适应思维（COGROUTER）显著提升了任务完成的效率和准确性，突显了动态推理在资源敏感任务中的价值。
