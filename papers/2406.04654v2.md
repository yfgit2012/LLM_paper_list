## Summary
- **Objective**: Develop no-reference image and video quality assessment (IQA/VQA) models (GenzIQA and GenzVQA) using diffusion models and cross-attention mechanisms to align visual features with human perception.  
- **Methodologies**:  
  - **GenzIQA**: Combines a diffusion model (Stable Diffusion) with cross-attention between text prompts (e.g., "high quality") and image features. Freezes query weights for robustness and trains key/value weights for adaptability.  
  - **GenzVQA**: Extends GenzIQA to videos, incorporating temporal dynamics and motion perception. Uses diffusion models for latent space analysis and cross-attention for spatial-temporal alignment.  
  - **Prompt Tuning**: Leverages text-based prompts to guide quality estimation, enhancing semantic alignment with human perception.  
  - **Training**: Trained on diverse datasets (e.g., KonIQ-10K, LIVE-IQA, KoNViD-1K) with single-step denoising for efficiency.  
- **Results**:  
  - **GenzIQA** outperforms LIQE, Re-IQA, and GRepQ on AI-generated and real-world image datasets, achieving SRCC > 0.8 on KonIQ-10K (SRCC = 0.820).  
  - **GenzVQA** surpasses ModularVQA and FastVQA on LSVQ and KoNViD-1K, with SRCC = 0.885 on KoNViD-1K.  
  - Ablation studies confirm that freezing query weights improves robustness, while training key/value weights enhances adaptability. Single-step denoising (t = 100) yields better performance than multi-step approaches.  
- **Key Contributions**:  
  - First integration of diffusion models and cross-attention for no-reference IQA/VQA.  
  - Prompt-based alignment between text and visual features for perceptual quality estimation.  
  - Cross-database generalization across diverse distortions (e.g., compression, motion blur).  
- **Conclusions**: GenzIQA and GenzVQA set new benchmarks for no-reference IQA/VQA by combining diffusion models with cross-attention and prompt tuning. They demonstrate strong performance on benchmark datasets and open avenues for lightweight deployment via distillation or inference acceleration.

## 摘要
- **目标**：利用扩散模型和交叉注意力机制开发无参考图像和视频质量评估（IQA/VQA）模型（GenzIQA 和 GenzVQA），以对齐视觉特征与人类感知。  
- **方法**：  
  - **GenzIQA**：结合扩散模型（Stable Diffusion）与文本提示（如“高质量”）和图像特征之间的交叉注意力。通过冻结查询权重以提高鲁棒性，并训练键/值权重以增强适应性。  
  - **GenzVQA**：将 GenzIQA 扩展到视频领域，引入时间动态和运动感知。利用扩散模型进行潜在空间分析，并通过交叉注意力实现时空对齐。  
  - **提示调优**：利用基于文本的提示引导质量估计，增强语义与人类感知的对齐。  
  - **训练**：在多样化数据集（如 KonIQ-10K、LIVE-IQA、KoNViD-1K）上训练，采用单步去噪以提高效率。  
- **结果**：  
  - **GenzIQA** 在 AI 生成图像和现实世界图像数据集上优于 LIQE、Re-IQA 和 GRepQ，在 KonIQ-10K 数据集上取得 SRCC > 0.8（SRCC = 0.820）。  
  - **GenzVQA** 在 LSVQ 和 KoNViD-1K 数据集上超越 ModularVQA 和 FastVQA，在 KoNViD-1K 数据集上 SRCC = 0.885。  
  - 消融实验验证了冻结查询权重可提升鲁棒性，而训练键/值权重可增强适应性。单步去噪（t = 100）比多步方法表现更优。  
- **关键贡献**：  
  - 首次将扩散模型与交叉注意力机制结合用于无参考 IQA/VQA。  
  - 基于提示的文本与视觉特征对齐，用于感知质量估计。  
  - 跨数据库的多样化失真（如压缩、运动模糊）泛化能力。  
- **结论**：GenzIQA 和 GenzVQA 通过结合扩散模型、交叉注意力和提示调优，为无参考 IQA/VQA 设立了新基准。它们在基准数据集上表现出色，并为轻量化部署（如知识蒸馏或推理加速）提供了可能性。
