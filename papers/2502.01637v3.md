## Summary  
- **Objective**: To provide a structured framework for researchers to ensure ethical, technical, and societal compliance when submitting work to conferences like NeurIPS.  
- **Methodologies**: A comprehensive checklist covering ethical compliance, broader societal impacts, safeguards for high-risk assets, reproducibility, licensing, human subject protocols, and LLM usage transparency.  
- **Results**: A detailed set of guidelines emphasizing proactive risk mitigation, reproducibility, and ethical accountability in AI research.  
- **Key Contributions**:  
  1. A standardized checklist for ethical and technical considerations.  
  2. Emphasis on balancing innovation with societal risks (e.g., disinformation, bias).  
  3. Practical steps for anonymization, licensing, and compute resource transparency.  
  4. Clear distinctions between core LLM usage and auxiliary applications.  
- **Conclusions**: Adhering to these guidelines ensures responsible AI research, fostering trust, reproducibility, and alignment with societal values.

## 摘要  
- **目标**：为研究人员提供一个结构化框架，确保在向NeurIPS等会议提交工作时符合伦理、技术和社会规范。  
- **方法**：涵盖伦理合规性、更广泛的社会影响、高风险资产保护、可重复性、授权许可、人类受试者协议及大语言模型（LLM）使用透明度的全面检查清单。  
- **结果**：一套详细的指南，强调人工智能研究中的主动风险缓解、可重复性及伦理问责。  
- **关键贡献**：  
  1. 伦理与技术考量的标准化检查清单。  
  2. 强调创新与社会风险之间的平衡（例如虚假信息、偏见）。  
  3. 匿名化、授权许可及计算资源透明度的实践步骤。  
  4. 核心LLM使用与辅助应用之间的明确区分。  
- **结论**：遵循这些指南可确保负责任的人工智能研究，促进信任、可重复性及与社会价值观的契合。
