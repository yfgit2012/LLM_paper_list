## Summary
- **Objective**: To address the challenges in assessing the quality of AI-generated and enhanced multimedia content (images and videos), emphasizing the need for robust quality assessment models that evaluate visual accuracy, user intent alignment, and user experience (QoE) beyond traditional metrics.  
- **Methodologies**: The paper employs objective quality assessment (e.g., FID, VMAF, CLIP scores) and subjective evaluation (ITU-T standards) to analyze GenAI content. It also explores datasets like COCO, ImageNet, and newly proposed ones (AGIQA-1K, AIGIQA-20K) and identifies limitations in current models for capturing semantic alignment, physical realism, and common sense.  
- **Results**: Existing datasets lack diversity and distortion types, limiting GenAI evaluation. Metrics like CLIP score and RAPIQUE show promise for semantic accuracy but fail to capture physical realism (e.g., "Mandalorian's missing leg"). Fusion-based models (e.g., COVER) and multimodal approaches (e.g., Vision-Language Models) are emerging trends. Challenges include temporal artifacts in video synthesis and the need for no-reference metrics for NeRF-based content.  
- **Key Contributions**: Identification of gaps in datasets and metrics for GenAI content; proposal of new evaluation frameworks incorporating perceptual, technical, and aesthetic aspects; highlighting the necessity for fusion-based and multimodal approaches.  
- **Conclusions**: Traditional IQA/VQA models are inadequate for GenAI content due to their focus on technical fidelity over perceptual and semantic quality. The paper advocates for standardized benchmarks, improved metrics for physical realism, and sustainable AI practices, emphasizing collaboration to ensure responsible AI deployment.

## 摘要
- **目标**：解决评估AI生成和增强多媒体内容（图像和视频）质量的挑战，强调需要构建稳健的质量评估模型，以评估视觉准确性、用户意图对齐及用户体验（QoE），而不仅限于传统指标。  
- **方法**：论文采用客观质量评估（如FID、VMAF、CLIP分数）和主观评价（ITU-T标准）分析GenAI内容。同时探讨了COCO、ImageNet等数据集以及新提出的AGIQA-1K、AIGIQA-20K等数据集，并指出现有模型在捕捉语义对齐、物理真实性和常识方面的局限性。  
- **结果**：现有数据集缺乏多样性和失真类型，限制了GenAI内容的评估。CLIP分数和RAPIQUE等指标在语义准确性方面表现良好，但无法捕捉物理真实性（例如“曼达洛人缺失的腿”）。基于融合的模型（如COVER）和多模态方法（如视觉-语言模型）成为新兴趋势。挑战包括视频合成中的时间伪影以及针对NeRF内容的无参考指标需求。  
- **关键贡献**：识别GenAI内容数据集和指标的不足；提出结合感知、技术和美学方面的新型评估框架；强调融合和多模态方法的必要性。  
- **结论**：传统IQA/VQA模型因过度关注技术保真度而无法满足GenAI内容的感知和语义质量需求。论文倡导建立标准化基准、改进物理真实性的评估指标，并推动可持续的AI实践，强调协作以确保负责任的AI部署。
