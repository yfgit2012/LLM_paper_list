## Summary  
- **Objective**: To develop a reinforcement learning method (Residual Flow Steering, RFS) for sim-to-real transfer in dexterous hand manipulation tasks, addressing challenges in bridging simulated training environments with real-world execution.  
- **Methodologies**:  
  - Combines **flow-based models** for action generation with **residual action correction** to improve robustness.  
  - Uses **PPO** for online training, **TD3+BC** for offline fine-tuning, and **teleoperation** (via Vision Pro and SpaceMouse) for human-in-the-loop corrections.  
  - Incorporates **disturbance injection**, **policy distillation**, and **point cloud data** to enhance generalization and mitigate hardware/camera errors.  
- **Results**:  
  - Demonstrated effective sim-to-real transfer through real-world experiments on grasping and pick-and-place tasks.  
  - Outperformed baselines like DPPO, ReinFlow, and FQL/IQL in robustness and task completion under disturbances.  
  - Achieved stable performance with human corrections limited to predefined thresholds (e.g., 1.5 cm translation, 0.05 rad finger motion).  
- **Key Contributions**:  
  - Proposes a **hybrid flow-residual framework** for sim-to-real transfer.  
  - Introduces **policy distillation with point clouds** and **disturbance injection** to improve real-world adaptability.  
  - Integrates **online PPO training** with **offline TD3+BC fine-tuning** for robust policy adaptation.  
  - Emphasizes **human-in-the-loop teleoperation** without shifting policy distributions.  
- **Conclusions**: RFS successfully bridges simulation and reality for dexterous robotic tasks by combining generative models, residual correction, and human feedback. The study highlights the importance of data diversity, disturbance resilience, and hybrid training strategies for practical robotic deployment.

## 摘要  
- **目标**：开发一种强化学习方法（残差流引导，RFS），用于灵巧手操作任务中的仿真至现实迁移，解决模拟训练环境与现实执行之间衔接的挑战。  
- **方法**：  
  - 将**基于流的模型**用于动作生成，结合**残差动作校正**以提升鲁棒性。  
  - 采用**PPO**进行在线训练，**TD3+BC**用于离线微调，并通过**遥操作系统**（Vision Pro 和 SpaceMouse）实现人机协同校正。  
  - 引入**扰动注入**、**点云数据**及**策略蒸馏**，以增强泛化能力并缓解硬件/摄像头误差。  
- **结果**：  
  - 通过现实场景中的抓取和搬运任务实验，验证了仿真至现实的迁移有效性。  
  - 在扰动环境下，鲁棒性与任务完成率优于基线方法（如 DPPO、ReinFlow、FQL/IQL）。  
  - 在人类校正限制在预定义阈值（如 1.5 厘米平移、0.05 弧度手指运动）下，实现了稳定性能。  
- **关键贡献**：  
  - 提出一种用于仿真至现实迁移的**混合流-残差框架**。  
  - 引入**点云数据的策略蒸馏**与**扰动注入**，提升现实适应性。  
  - 将**在线 PPO 训练**与**离线 TD3+BC 微调**结合，实现鲁棒策略适应。  
  - 强调**人机协同遥操作系统**，无需改变策略分布。  
- **结论**：RFS 通过结合生成模型、残差校正和人类反馈，成功实现灵巧机器人任务中仿真与现实的衔接。研究强调了数据多样性、抗干扰能力及混合训练策略在实际机器人部署中的重要性。
