## Summary  
- **Objective**: To evaluate the effectiveness of different agent-human simulator configurations (GPT-4o, GPT-4.1, and Llama-4-Scout) in achieving task success, personalization accuracy, and feedback efficiency.  
- **Methodologies**: Experimental comparisons using SQLite as a memory system, tracking metrics such as success rate, feedback frequency, and ACPE (Average Cumulative Personalization Error) across iterative testing phases. Data was analyzed to assess performance trends and stability.  
- **Results**:  
  - Success rates improved with iterations, indicating adaptive learning.  
  - ACPE values decreased over time, suggesting better personalization accuracy.  
  - Feedback frequency stabilized, implying reduced reliance on external input as tasks were mastered.  
  - GPT-4o demonstrated higher success rates and lower ACPE compared to GPT-4.1 when paired with Llama-4-Scout.  
- **Key Contributions**:  
  - Introduction of ACPE as a metric for evaluating personalization tasks in agent-human interactions.  
  - Comparative analysis of GPT-4o and GPT-4.1 with Llama-4-Scout, highlighting performance differences.  
  - Demonstration of iterative learning trends in AI/ML systems using structured experimental setups.  
- **Conclusions**: GPT-4o outperformed GPT-4.1 in task success and personalization accuracy when paired with Llama-4-Scout, underscoring its effectiveness in dynamic agent-human simulator configurations. The study emphasizes the importance of iterative testing and metric-driven analysis in AI system optimization.

## 摘要  
- **目标**：评估不同代理-人类模拟器配置（GPT-4o、GPT-4.1 和 Llama-4-Scout）在任务成功率、个性化准确性及反馈效率方面的有效性。  
- **方法**：使用 SQLite 作为记忆系统进行实验对比，跟踪迭代测试阶段中的成功率、反馈频率及 ACPE（平均累计个性化误差）等指标，分析数据以评估性能趋势和稳定性。  
- **结果**：  
  - 成功率随迭代次数增加而提升，表明具备自适应学习能力。  
  - ACPE 值随时间下降，说明个性化准确性提高。  
  - 反馈频率趋于稳定，表明任务掌握后对外部输入的依赖减少。  
  - 当与 Llama-4-Scout 配对时，GPT-4o 的成功率更高且 ACPE 更低。  
- **关键贡献**：  
  - 引入 ACPE 作为评估代理-人类交互中个性化任务的指标。  
  - 对 GPT-4o 和 GPT-4.1 与 Llama-4-Scout 的对比分析，突出其性能差异。  
  - 通过结构化实验设置展示 AI/ML 系统的迭代学习趋势。  
- **结论**：当与 Llama-4-Scout 配对时，GPT-4o 在任务成功率和个性化准确性方面优于 GPT-4.1，凸显其在动态代理-人类模拟器配置中的有效性。研究强调了迭代测试和指标驱动分析在 AI 系统优化中的重要性。
