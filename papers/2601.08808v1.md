## Summary  
- **Objective**: To evaluate the effectiveness of **Multiplex Thinking** as an advanced reasoning approach compared to traditional **Discrete Reinforcement Learning (Discrete RL)**, with a focus on improving robustness and coverage in reasoning tasks.  
- **Methodologies**: The study contrasts Discrete RL (sequential, single-path reasoning) with Multiplex Thinking (parallel reasoning paths controlled by parameter *K*). Experiments were conducted on datasets like MATH-500, using metrics such as **Pass@k** and validation scores. Ablation studies analyzed the impact of varying *K* values.  
- **Results**: Multiplex Thinking (K ≥ 2) outperformed Discrete RL (K=1) across all metrics, with the most significant gains at lower *k* (e.g., Pass@1). K=3 achieved the best balance between performance and efficiency, while higher K values showed diminishing returns. Training dynamics revealed faster convergence and stability for Multiplex models.  
- **Key Contributions**:  
  1. Introduction of **Multiplex Thinking** as a novel framework for parallel hypothesis exploration.  
  2. Empirical validation of the **K parameter’s trade-off** between performance and computational cost.  
  3. Demonstration of improved **robustness and coverage** in complex reasoning tasks.  
- **Conclusions**: Multiplex Thinking with *K=3* optimally balances performance and efficiency, outperforming Discrete RL. Parallel reasoning paths enhance robustness without excessive computational overhead, offering a promising direction for advancing AI reasoning systems.

## 摘要  
- **目标**：评估**多路思维**作为一种高级推理方法相较于传统**离散强化学习（Discrete RL）**的有效性，重点提升推理任务中的鲁棒性和覆盖范围。  
- **方法**：研究对比了离散强化学习（顺序、单路径推理）与多路思维（由参数*K*控制的并行推理路径）。实验在MATH-500等数据集上进行，使用**Pass@k**和验证得分等指标。消融实验分析了不同*K*值的影响。  
- **结果**：多路思维（*K* ≥ 2）在所有指标上均优于离散强化学习（*K*=1），尤其在较低的*k*（如Pass@1）时提升显著。*K*=3在性能与效率之间达到最佳平衡，而更高*K*值的边际效益递减。训练动态显示多路模型收敛速度更快且更稳定。  
- **关键贡献**：  
  1. 引入**多路思维**作为并行假设探索的新框架。  
  2. 实证验证了**K参数**在性能与计算成本之间的权衡关系。  
  3. 展示了复杂推理任务中**鲁棒性与覆盖范围**的提升。  
- **结论**：采用*K*=3的多路思维在性能与效率之间达到最佳平衡，优于离散强化学习。并行推理路径在提升鲁棒性的同时避免了过高的计算开销，为推进AI推理系统提供了有前景的方向。
