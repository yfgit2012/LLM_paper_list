## Summary
- **Objective**: To predict the next product in a user’s hair care routine based on their purchase history, leveraging sequential patterns and product categorization.  
- **Methodologies**: Analyzed purchase sequences to identify patterns in hair care routines (shampoo → conditioner → styling/treatment). Used product categorization (e.g., “Beauty > Hair Care”) and brand/product type alignment to recommend the next logical step in the routine.  
- **Results**: The user’s purchase history (cleanser and conditioner) indicated a sequential pattern of shampoo → conditioner → styling/treatment. Candidate 1 (One ’n Only Argan Oil Styling Cream) was identified as the most logical next product due to brand alignment and sequence consistency.  
- **Key Contributions**: Introduced a framework for predicting sequential product recommendations in beauty routines by combining purchase history analysis with category-based inference. Highlighted the importance of brand and product type alignment in personalized recommendations.  
- **Conclusions**: Sequential purchase patterns and product categorization can effectively predict the next step in a user’s hair care routine, enabling more accurate and personalized recommendations.

## 摘要
- **目标**：基于用户的购买历史，利用顺序模式和产品分类预测其护发流程中的下一步产品。  
- **方法**：分析购买序列以识别护发流程中的模式（洗发水 → 护发素 → 型/护理产品）。通过产品分类（例如，“美容 > 护发”）及品牌/产品类型匹配，推荐流程中的下一步产品。  
- **结果**：用户的购买历史（清洁剂和护发素）表明存在洗发水 → 护发素 → 定型/护理产品的顺序模式。候选产品1（One 'n Only 阿甘油定型乳）因品牌匹配和顺序一致性被确定为最合理的下一步产品。  
- **关键贡献**：通过结合购买历史分析与基于分类的推理，提出一种预测美容流程中顺序产品推荐的框架。强调了品牌和产品类型匹配在个性化推荐中的重要性。  
- **结论**：顺序购买模式和产品分类可有效预测用户护发流程中的下一步，从而实现更精准的个性化推荐。
