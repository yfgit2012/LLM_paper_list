## Summary  
- **Objective**: To explore the "unreasonable effectiveness of mathematics in the natural sciences" as conceptualized by Wigner, while integrating modern developments in AI, computational models, and philosophical debates to reassess its validity, limitations, and cultural context.  
- **Methodologies**: A structured analysis combining philosophical inquiry, case studies of AI advancements (e.g., neurosymbolic AI, transformers), empirical studies on cognitive biases and problem-solving, and interdisciplinary insights from indigenous knowledge systems.  
- **Results**: The paper highlights the enduring effectiveness of mathematics in modeling natural phenomena, validated by AI systems like LLMs and neurosymbolic frameworks. However, it also identifies limitations, such as the inability of mathematical models to fully capture complex or emergent phenomena, and challenges the universality of mathematics by emphasizing culturally shaped mathematical intuition.  
- **Key Contributions**:  
  1. Bridges Wigner’s original argument with contemporary AI and computational paradigms.  
  2. Introduces neurosymbolic AI as a modern extension of mathematics’ role in science.  
  3. Critiques the universality of mathematics by incorporating indigenous knowledge and cognitive biases.  
  4. Reconciles ontic and epistemic realism through the lens of AI’s hybrid symbolic-statistical approaches.  
- **Conclusions**: The "unreasonable effectiveness" of mathematics arises from its adaptability, human creativity, and empirical grounding. Future research should integrate symbolic logic, statistical learning, and cultural perspectives to refine mathematics’ role in science. The paper underscores that mathematics is both a human construct and a flexible tool for navigating empirical complexity.

## 摘要  
- **目标**：探讨威格纳所提出的“数学在自然科学中的非理性有效性”，结合现代人工智能、计算模型及哲学争论，重新评估其有效性、局限性及文化背景。  
- **方法**：通过哲学探究、人工智能进展案例研究（如神经符号AI、Transformer模型）、关于认知偏差与问题解决的实证研究，以及跨学科的原住民知识体系见解，进行结构化分析。  
- **结果**：论文强调数学在建模自然现象中的持久有效性，这一有效性通过大型语言模型（LLMs）和神经符号框架等AI系统得到验证。然而，也指出了数学模型在完全捕捉复杂或涌现现象方面的局限性，并通过强调文化塑造的数学直觉，挑战数学的普适性。  
- **主要贡献**：  
  1. 将威格纳的原始论点与当代人工智能和计算范式相衔接。  
  2. 引入神经符号AI作为数学在科学中作用的现代延伸。  
  3. 通过融入原住民知识和认知偏差，批判数学的普适性。  
  4. 通过AI混合符号-统计方法的视角，调和本体论与认识论的实在论。  
- **结论**：数学的“非理性有效性”源于其适应性、人类创造力和经验基础。未来研究应整合符号逻辑、统计学习和文化视角，以完善数学在科学中的角色。论文强调，数学既是人类构建的产物，也是应对经验复杂性的灵活工具。
