## Summary  
- **Objective**: To solve the problem of finding the maximum absolute difference between the total weights of any two trucks, where each truck consists of exactly `k` consecutive boxes (with `k` dividing `n`).  
- **Methodologies**: The approach involves iterating over all valid `k` values (divisors of `n`), splitting the array into consecutive groups of size `k`, computing group sums, and tracking the maximum and minimum sums to calculate the difference. The overall maximum difference across all valid `k` is returned.  
- **Results**: The algorithm correctly computes the maximum difference by evaluating all valid groupings. For example, with `n=4` and weights `[1,2,3,4]`, the maximum difference is achieved when `k=2` (difference of 4). The time complexity is O(n²), which is acceptable for small to moderate input sizes.  
- **Key Contributions**: The paper presents a correct and straightforward approach for consecutive grouping of boxes, emphasizing the importance of checking divisibility and efficiently computing group sums. It also highlights the trade-off between simplicity and performance for larger inputs.  
- **Conclusions**: The solution adheres to the problem’s constraints, ensuring correctness through consecutive grouping and divisibility checks. While the O(n²) complexity is acceptable for small `n`, the paper notes potential optimizations for larger datasets.

## 摘要  
- **目标**：解决在每辆卡车由恰好 `k` 个连续的箱子组成（其中 `k` 是 `n` 的因数）的情况下，找出任意两辆卡车总重量的绝对差值的最大值。  
- **方法**：通过遍历所有有效的 `k` 值（即 `n` 的因数），将数组分割为连续的 `k` 个元素的子数组，计算每个子数组的和，并记录最大值和最小值以求得差值。最终返回所有有效 `k` 对应的最大差值。  
- **结果**：该算法通过评估所有有效分组正确计算出最大差值。例如，当 `n=4` 且重量为 `[1,2,3,4]` 时，当 `k=2` 时差值达到最大（差值为 4）。时间复杂度为 O(n²)，适用于小规模或中等规模的输入。  
- **关键贡献**：论文提出了一种正确且直接的连续分组方法，强调了检查因数关系和高效计算子数组和的重要性。同时，也指出了在更大输入规模下简单性与性能之间的权衡。  
- **结论**：该解决方案符合问题约束条件，通过连续分组和因数检查确保了正确性。尽管 O(n²) 的复杂度对小规模 `n` 是可接受的，但论文指出对于更大数据集可能存在优化空间。
