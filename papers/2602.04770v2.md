## Summary
- **Objective**: To develop a drift-based generative model for high-quality image generation, competing with the improved MeanFlow (iMF) method by optimizing realism and diversity through latent space drift and configuration guidance (CFG).  
- **Methodologies**: The model employs a latent space drift mechanism to guide diffusion processes via learned drift vectors, combined with CFG to balance prior and data distributions. It uses DiT (Diffusion Transformer) variants (e.g., DiT-L/2, DiT-XL/2) for image generation.  
- **Results**: The proposed model achieves **FID = 1.54** (vs. iMF's 3.92) and **IS = 258.9** (vs. iMF's 348.2) at CFG=1.5, demonstrating slightly better realism while matching diversity. Visual comparisons across ImageNet classes (e.g., house finch, white wolf, coral reef) show competitive quality and diversity.  
- **Key Contributions**: Introduces a drift-based generative approach with CFG optimization for balancing realism and diversity, achieving state-of-the-art performance on ImageNet. Highlights the trade-off between FID and IS through hyperparameter tuning.  
- **Conclusions**: The model outperforms iMF in realism while maintaining diversity, showcasing its potential for high-quality image generation. Future work could explore scalability, training efficiency, and modality extensions (e.g., text-to-image).

## 摘要
- **目标**：开发一种基于漂移的生成模型，用于高质量图像生成，通过潜在空间漂移和配置引导（CFG）优化真实性和多样性，与改进的MeanFlow（iMF）方法竞争。  
- **方法**：该模型采用潜在空间漂移机制，通过学习到的漂移向量引导扩散过程，并结合CFG平衡先验分布与数据分布。其使用DiT（扩散变换器）变体（如DiT-L/2、DiT-XL/2）进行图像生成。  
- **结果**：所提模型在CFG=1.5时达到**FID = 1.54**（对比iMF的3.92）和**IS = 258.9**（对比iMF的348.2），在提升真实性的同时保持多样性。在ImageNet类别（如房屋雀、白狼、珊瑚礁）的视觉对比中，显示了具有竞争力的质量和多样性。

- **结论**：该模型在保持多样性的同时优于iMF的真实感，展示了其在高质量图像生成中的潜力。未来工作可探索可扩展性、训练效率及模态扩展（如文本到图像）。
