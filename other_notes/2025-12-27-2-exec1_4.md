## Think Like an Executive: The Mental Model of Decision-Makers

The document outlines five key principles to help individuals transition from being individual contributors (ICs) to executives. Here's a concise summary of each principle:

1. **Think Like An Owner (TLAO)**:
   - Develop an ownership mindset by focusing on long-term goals, strategic decisions, and calculated risks rather than short-term gains or task completion.

2. **Orient to Outcomes**:
   - Shift focus from completing tasks to achieving desired results. Understand how each initiative contributes to the broader organizational vision and success metrics.

3. **Work with Wisdom**:
   - Cultivate discernment in decision-making, prioritizing efforts that yield maximum impact. Learn to allocate resources effectively and understand trade-offs between different strategic choices.

4. **Navigate Trade-offs**:
   - Embrace the reality of business trade-offs by making informed decisions that consider both pros and cons. Understand that not all decisions will be perfect but focus on minimizing negative impacts while maximizing benefits.

5. **Embody Stewardship**:
   - Adopt a mindset of responsibility towards resources and organizational legacy. View your role as a privilege to contribute to the company's long-term success, rather than just another job.

By internalizing these principles, individuals can transition from task-focused execution to strategic leadership, fostering a mindset aligned with executive responsibilities.

1. **像所有者一样思考（TLAO）**：

   - 将焦点从完成任务转移到实现预期结果。了解每个举措如何为组织的总体愿景和成功指标做出贡献。

3. **智慧地工作**：
   - 培养在决策中展现 discernment 的能力，优先考虑那些能产生最大影响的努力。学会有效地分配资源，并理解不同战略选择之间的权衡。

4. **应对权衡**：
   - 接受商业权衡的现实，通过综合考虑利弊来做出明智决定。认识到并非所有决定都是完美的，但重点在于最小化负面影响并最大化利益。

5. **体现管家角色**：
   - 采取一种对资源和组织遗产负责的心态。将你的角色视为一种 privilege，以促进公司的长期成功，而不仅仅是另一份工作。

通过内化这些原则，个人可以实现从任务执行到战略性领导的转型，培养与高管责任相一致的心态。</document>
