## Feeling Invisible at Work? Get Attention With G.R.O.W.T.H.

### Summary:
The article discusses a six-part framework called "GROWTH" to help individuals become more visible to senior executives or key figures they want to impress at work. The framework emphasizes strategies such as giving credit to others, reaching out to influencers, operating from your highest values, working on continuous learning, tracking your progress, and honing your expertise. The article highlights the importance of aligning your actions with what senior leaders value most, building meaningful relationships, and consistently demonstrating your worth through self-awareness, growth, and clear communication.

---

### Key Points:
- **Invisibility at Work**: Feeling invisible is often due to a lack of attention from senior executives.
- **GROWTH Framework**:
  - **G**: Give credit to others. Focus on developing team members and speaking in terms of leaders' priorities.
  - **R**: Reach out to influencers. Identify key decision-makers, understand their values, and build equitable relationships.
  - **O**: Operate from your highest value. Align your work with what matters most to you and live authentically.
  - **W**: Work on continuous learning. Transform information into actionable knowledge rather than just consuming it.
  - **T**: Track your progress. Report regularly on leading metrics to advocate for yourself effectively.
  - **H**: Hone your expertise. Articulate your knowledge clearly so others can understand and value your contributions.
