## Summary

MIT researchers developed the SEAL (Self-Adapting Language Models) paper, which enables LLMs to autonomously generate training data, fine-tune themselves, and improve without human intervention, showcasing a clever self-evolution approach. The paper does not claim GPT-6 is alive, despite the provocative title suggestion.
