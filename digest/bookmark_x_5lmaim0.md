## Summary

A new study reveals that state-of-the-art LLMs struggle with "Theory of Mind" and "Theory of Space," exhibiting "Belief Inertia" by refusing to update their understanding of the world despite clear changes. This highlights their limitations in modeling others' knowledge and spatial relationships.
