## Summary

A blogger replicated DeepSeek's mHC paper, achieving 10,924x signal amplification using a 1.7B parameter model, with results surpassing the paper's reported outcomes. The experiments, conducted on 8 H100 GPUs over 18 trials, compared three architectures: standard residual connections, unconstrained Hyper-Connections (HC), and mHC with Sinkhorn projection.
