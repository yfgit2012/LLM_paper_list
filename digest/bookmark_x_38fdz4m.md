## Summary

On-Policy Self-Distillation (OPSD) is a technique where a single model acts as both teacher and student, using a privileged, better version of itself to self-criticize and refine its reasoning through self-distillation. This process enables models to improve their performance by comparing their outputs to a superior version of their own behavior.

**Category:** Model training and reinforcement learning
