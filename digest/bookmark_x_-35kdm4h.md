## Summary

Midtraining, a growing component of training pipelines, can enhance learning when it effectively bridges pretraining and posttraining phases while reducing forgetting, as shown in a new preprint study through controlled experiments. The key benefit lies in its ability to mitigate knowledge loss during the transition between training stages.
