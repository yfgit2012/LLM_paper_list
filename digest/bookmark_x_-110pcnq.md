## Summary

The paper introduces "Rewards as Labels (REAL)," a framework that reclassifies reward signals as categorical labels, transforming policy optimization into a classification task within the RLVR paradigm. This approach revisits reinforcement learning by treating rewards as discrete labels rather than continuous weights, enabling a new perspective on reward-based decision-making.
