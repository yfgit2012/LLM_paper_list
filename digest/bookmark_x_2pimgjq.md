## Summary

MaxRL is a streamlined modification to GRPO that replaces the expected reward objective with maximum likelihood optimization, leveraging scaling via 1/μ instead of traditional 1/σ to enhance learning efficiency. This approach enables faster progress from low initial performance levels compared to REINFORCE or standard GRPO.

**Category:** Model training and reinforcement learning
