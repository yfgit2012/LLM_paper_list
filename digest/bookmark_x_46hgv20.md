Agent Data Protocol (ADP) has been accepted for an oral presentation at ICLR 2026, and the team now supports three new datasets (SWE-Play, MiniCoder, and Toucan), expanding supported trajectories to 3 million. Researchers training agentic LMs are encouraged to try ADP and share their dataset/agent experiences.
