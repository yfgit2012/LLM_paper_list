## Summary

A new study found that students interacting with an LLM using an empathic system prompt experienced stronger emotional responses like empathy and compassion compared to those using a compassionate AI or text-only controls, while knowledge gains remained similar. This highlights the impact of empathic framing on affective engagement with AI.
