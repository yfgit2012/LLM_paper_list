## Summary

The tweet introduces **Reinforced Attention Learning**, a novel approach in reinforcement learning for LLMs that shifts focus from optimizing generated outputs to directing where the model allocates attention, aiming to influence its reasoning process more effectively. This contrasts with traditional methods that prioritize what is generated over how the model processes information.

**Category:** Model training and reinforcement learning
