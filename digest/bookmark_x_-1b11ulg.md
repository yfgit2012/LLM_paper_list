## Summary

Researchers from HKUST, CASIA, and UC Santa Cruz introduced LoopViT, a compact AI model that uses looped reasoning with reused weights to outperform larger models, challenging the notion that bigger AI is always better. This "looped" transformer approach enables efficient, iterative reasoning without scaling up size.
