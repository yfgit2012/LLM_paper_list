## Summary

A new Microsoft-Penn State paper introduces "Multiplex Thinking," a method where LLMs sample multiple next tokens per step and merge them into a single internal token, enabling them to solve complex math problems more efficiently than traditional chain-of-thought approaches. This technique allows LLMs to tackle harder problems with shorter, more optimized reasoning paths.
