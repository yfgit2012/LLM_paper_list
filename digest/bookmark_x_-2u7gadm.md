## Summary

Tencent and Tsinghua University introduced CALM, a new language model that replaces the traditional "next-token" prediction method with "next-vectors" to overcome efficiency bottlenecks in current LLMs. This shift aims to streamline prediction by processing continuous vector representations instead of individual word pieces.
