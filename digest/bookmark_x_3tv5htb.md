## Summary

OpenAI launched Prism, a free AI workspace for scientists, combining a cloud-based LaTeX editor with GPT-5.2, after acquiring the LaTeX platform Crixet to integrate its advanced model. This tool aims to streamline scientific writing and analysis by merging AI-powered text generation with LaTeX's technical formatting capabilities.
