## Summary

A new DeepMind paper introduces "Unified Latents (UL)," a method to automatically train latent representations in diffusion models, eliminating the need for manual tuning of latent information capacity. This addresses the challenge of determining optimal latent settings in latent diffusion models, which previously required guesswork.
