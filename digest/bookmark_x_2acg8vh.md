## Summary

Dr. Tulu's RL with evolving rubrics (RLER) represents a significant advancement for Rubrics-as-Rewards (RaR), aiming to extend reinforcement learning to non-verifiable domains by replacing deterministic rules with adaptable rubrics. This approach seeks to generalize RL systems beyond verifiable rewards, enhancing their applicability in complex, real-world scenarios.
