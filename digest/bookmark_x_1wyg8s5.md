## Summary

The paper introduces a novel approach to On-Policy Distillation by having the policy act as both teacher and student, using a "golden trajectory" to condition the teacher and enabling self-supervised learning through policy training. This dual-role framework enhances efficiency in reinforcement learning by eliminating the need for separate teacher models.
