## Summary

Meta has addressed a major limitation in Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) by introducing REFRAG, which reduces unnecessary computational costs by efficiently retrieving only relevant information instead of processing excessive, irrelevant data. Traditional RAG systems waste resources by retrieving too many chunks and forcing LLMs to process irrelevant tokens, leading to higher costs.
